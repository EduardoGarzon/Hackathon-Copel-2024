I participated in the Copel Hackathon – Challenges in Customer Service, an event that brought together universities from Paraná to develop innovative solutions focused on digital transformation and improving the consumer experience in the electricity sector. The challenge consisted of proposing a strategic solution to optimize customer service, reduce operational costs, and improve communication in scenarios such as major weather events, asset monitoring, loss reduction, and energy consumption management. The proposal was built on the integration of the Smart Grid system with digital service channels, such as mobile applications and WhatsApp, aiming for greater efficiency, scalability, and reduced dependence on human assistance.
***
The developed solution integrated Smart Grid data to generate real-time insights into energy consumption, power outages, and irregularities, using automated communication via Chatbot (Lúcio) and WhatsApp. The model included sending automatic notifications about overdue bills, alerts about consumption anomalies, updates on repair complexity (high, medium, or low), and incentives for adopting digital billing. The proposal included customer service automation, omnichannel integration, intelligent redirection to the mobile app, and reduced demand for call centers and in-person service, promoting greater digital engagement. An economic feasibility analysis was also structured, demonstrating a significant reduction in costs by replacing human service with automated channels, in addition to strategic modeling using Business Model Canvas.  
***
As a result, I developed skills in modeling scalable digital solutions, data analysis for generating insights, digital transformation strategy, optimization of customer service processes, reduction of operational costs, and integration between systems. The experience strengthened skills in systemic business vision, financial impact analysis, structuring technical and strategic proposals, multidisciplinary teamwork, and executive presentation of solutions focused on operational efficiency, innovation, and improvement of the customer experience (CX).
